Frequently Asked Questions

What is PsyAssist?
PsyAssist is an AI-powered mental health support assistant designed to provide emotional support, coping strategies, and mental health resources. It uses advanced AI technology to understand your concerns and provide helpful responses based on psychological principles.
Is my conversation private?
Yes, your privacy is important to us. All conversations are encrypted and securely stored. Your data is only used to improve the quality of responses and is not shared with third parties. You can request deletion of your chat history at any time.
Is PsyAssist a replacement for therapy?
No, PsyAssist is not a replacement for professional mental health treatment. While it can provide support and resources, it should be used as a complementary tool. If you're experiencing severe distress or have a mental health condition, please consult with a licensed mental health professional.
How does PsyAssist work?
PsyAssist uses advanced AI technology to understand your messages and provide supportive responses. It analyzes the emotional content of your messages and draws on psychological resources to give appropriate and helpful responses. The system is designed to be empathetic, non-judgmental, and focused on your wellbeing.
What should I do in a crisis situation?
If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please contact emergency services (911 in the US) right away. For mental health crises, you can also contact:
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Your local emergency mental health services
Can I use PsyAssist in languages other than English?
PsyAssist primarily operates in English but can understand and respond in several other languages. For best results, we recommend using English, but feel free to communicate in your preferred language.
